## 💡 Problem Statement

Many students are confused about:
- What career to choose
- What skills to learn
- How much effort is needed
- What salary they can expect

---

## ✅ Solution

PathPilot AI+ solves this by:
- Generating a **personalized roadmap**
- Predicting **salary based on study hours**
- Suggesting **realistic companies to target**
- Providing **actionable career guidance**

---

## 🔥 Features

- 🎯 Career Role Recommendation  
- 🧠 Skills Roadmap (Beginner → Advanced)  
- 🛠 Tools & Technologies  
- 💼 Industry-Level Project Ideas  
- 📅 30-Day Learning Plan  
- 💰 Salary Prediction (based on effort)  
- 📈 Growth Insights  
- 🏢 Target Companies with career links  
- 📄 Resume Tips  

---

## 🧠 How It Works

User provides:
- Branch  
- Interests  
- Skill Level  
- Weekly Study Hours  
- Career Goal  

AI generates a **complete career intelligence report**.
